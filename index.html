Dr Adam Goodge

Scientist
I2R, A*STAR

E-mail: adamgoodge@hotmail.com

I'm a scientist at the Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R), Agency of Science of Technology and Research of Singapore (A*STAR). I was a PhD student in the School of Computing at the National University of Singapore and recipient of the Singapore International Graduate Award (SINGA) scholarship from 2018-2022. My expertise is in state-of-the-art deep-learning techniques for the challenging task of anomaly detection in various applications. I am also interested in developments in foundation models, generative AI and multi-modality such as vision-language modelling.
